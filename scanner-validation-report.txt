4. Detection patterns that looked reliable
------------------------------------------
- Shopify detections appear reliable when Shopify asset/script patterns are present and a detection confidence is high.
- GA4 and GTM detection is reliable for sites with visible gtag, GTM IDs, or dataLayer setup.
- Meta Pixel is reliably detected when fbq or facebook tracking script patterns exist.

5. Detection patterns that need improvement
---------------------------------------------
- BigCommerce and Magento / Adobe Commerce false negatives: several sites returned Unknown despite expected platform definitions.
- WooCommerce and custom storefronts may require stronger HTML/UI heuristics beyond scripts and paths.
- Platform detection confidence is too low for sites with minimal visible script metadata.

6. Recommended next fixes ranked by product impact
--------------------------------------------------
- Improve BigCommerce/Magento storefront detection heuristics first, because many failed or mismatched enterprise sites depend on these signals.
- Add better Unknown handling and fallback rules for sites with hidden or deferred scripts.
- Strengthen cart/checkout visibility patterns to reduce false negatives on commerce flow detection.
- Tune marketing tool detection for non-standard tag injection flows (e.g. GTM containerless setups).
